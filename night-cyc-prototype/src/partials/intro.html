Sweat, Music and Dance ...

Imagine one night, one commUNITY coming together to make children's dreams come alive at the Ryan House. We can't imagine a better way to embrace children and their families as they navigate their unique life-limiting or end-of-life journeys than to do this togther at the amazing Madison Improvement Club.

Join us for a post-sweat after party hosted by "the coup" at Culinary Dropout where you will receive complimentary drinks and light snacks.

The studios and classes ...

This event will be held Saturday January 23rd at The Madison in Tempe Arizona. There will be 4 studios putting on 5 hours of classes:

  • 3-4 pm by Andrew Varela and Michael Collinge - The Madison (Tempe)
  • 4-5 pm by Ambassador Bad Asses
  • 5-6 pm by Matt Peterson and Lindsay Reuben - The Madison (Phoenix)
  • 6-7 pm by Holly Miller - RPM spin
  • 7-8 pm by Brittny Golding and Mikayla Shannon - The Madison (Tempe)
